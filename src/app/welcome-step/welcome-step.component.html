This application supports you with up-to-date information and knowledge on surgical techniques when choosing the one for your patient.
You will be guided step by step through the necessary selection steps: Selection of the location and size of the lesion will propose you fitting surgical techniques.
You can also draw the patient's lesion and the application will propose known lesion types and related surgical techniques.
